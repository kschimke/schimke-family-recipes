Ingredients:

- Sugar, 1/2 tbsp
- Instant coffee, 1/2 tbsp
- Hot water, 1/2 tbsp
- Milk, 1 cup

Steps:

- Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
- Whip the mixture vigorously.
- Serve the whipped coffee over milk.
